Fairblock provides end-to-end confidential computing for blockchains. By default amounts and balances are encrypted and protcted while addresses stay transparent, enabling compliant privacy across DeFi, payments, and institutional finance. For specific institutional deployments that require stronger privacy, Fairblock can also support fully compliant unlinkable address flows where counterparties need protection from public relationship mapping. Examples include merchant payments, treasury management, VC fund operations, trading desks, and banking applications.

The network operates as a decentralized cryptographic network built on [FairyRing](/protocol/fairyring), with native support for EVM chains such as Arbitrum, Base, Tempo, Arc, Ethereum, HyperEVM, Stable, BNB as well as Stellar and Solana.
